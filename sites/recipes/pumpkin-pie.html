Ingredients

For the dough

1 tbs of water
2 cups of water
2 eggs
1 cup of sugar

For the filling

1 tbs of water
2 cups of flour
2 eggs
1 cup of sugar

The method

To make a pumpkin pie, start by mixing 1 ½ cups of flour and ½ teaspoon of salt, then cut in ½ cup of cold butter until crumbly; add 4-5 tablespoons of ice water to form a dough, chill for an hour, and roll it out into a pie pan. For the filling, whisk together one 15-ounce can of pumpkin puree, ¾ cup of sugar, 1 teaspoon of cinnamon, ½ teaspoon of ginger, ¼ teaspoon each of nutmeg and cloves, and ½ teaspoon of salt, then mix in 3 eggs and 1 cup of heavy cream. Pour the filling into the crust, bake at 425°F for 15 minutes, then reduce to 350°F and bake for 35-40 minutes until set. Let it cool before serving, and enjoy!
